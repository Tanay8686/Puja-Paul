Best Term Paper Award

3rd Position

Along with my team, I presented a project titled "Thermal Management of the Outer Shell of a Coaxial Cylinder Containing a Constant Flux Heat Source Using P, PI, and PID Controllers" as part of our Control Engineering course. The project focused on optimizing the thermal management system of a coaxial cylinder by utilizing P, PI, and PID controllers to regulate the temperature of the outer shell effectively. Through modeling and simulation, we evaluated the performance of each control strategy and its practical applications in engineering.
