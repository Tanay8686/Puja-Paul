INTRODUCTION

I am a recent graduate in Mechanical Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), where I established a solid foundation in engineering principles and practices. My academic journey has ignited a passion for research and experimentation across a diverse range of engineering fields, including Advanced Materials, Additive Manufacturing, Thermo-fluid Dynamics, and various innovative manufacturing processes. As a PhD aspirant, I am eager to explore new frontiers in engineering that contribute to technological advancements and practical solutions.

Currently, my research focuses on understanding how process parameters influence materials performance, employing both experimental techniques and simulations. For my undergraduate thesis, I conducted a comprehensive investigation into the acoustic and thermal properties of locally available particle boards, successfully merging theoretical concepts with practical application. Additionally, I have gained proficiency in Computational Fluid Dynamics through a project on the thermal management of flowing fluid, utilizing P, PI, and PID controllers.

I am skilled in a variety of engineering software tools, including Lammps, Matlab, Ansys, SolidWorks, AutoCAD and COMSOL, which empower me to model and analyze complex engineering challenges. Furthermore, I possess foundational knowledge of programming languages such as Python and C, enhancing my ability to tackle computational tasks effectively.

As a smart, ambitious, and industrious individual, I am dedicated to continuous learning and innovation in the engineering field. I am excited to share my research, insights, and projects through this website. Thank you for stopping by, and I look forward to connecting with fellow enthusiasts in the field!
